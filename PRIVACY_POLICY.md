# Privacy Policy for Vista Running

**Effective Date**: January 17, 2025  
**Last Updated**: January 17, 2025

## Introduction

Vista Running ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Vista Running (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Running Activity Data
We collect the following information about your running activities:
- **Distance**: Miles or kilometers of your runs
- **Pace**: Your running pace per mile/kilometer
- **Time**: Duration of your running sessions
- **Run Type**: Type of workout (easy run, tempo, intervals, etc.)
- **Notes**: Personal notes about your runs
- **Date**: When you performed your runs

**Purpose**: This data is collected to help you track your running progress, analyze your performance, and maintain your personal training log.

### 2. App Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage data including:
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version
- **App Performance**: Crash reports and performance metrics
- **Feature Usage**: Which features you use most frequently

**Purpose**: This data helps us improve the app's performance and user experience.

### 3. Data We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data or GPS tracking
- Photos or camera access
- Contact information
- Payment information
- Health data beyond what you manually enter

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Personal Training Log**: Display your running history and progress
- **Analytics**: Show charts and trends of your running performance
- **Data Backup**: Securely store your data to prevent loss
- **App Improvement**: Enhance features based on anonymous usage patterns

### Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: Your running data is primarily stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Backup**: We may offer optional cloud backup for data security
- **Security**: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT Share Your Data
Vista Running does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal running data to third parties. Your running logs and personal notes remain private.

### Limited Exceptions
We may disclose information only in these circumstances:
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or court order
- **Safety**: To protect the safety of our users or the public
- **Business Transfer**: In the event of a merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control
- **Access**: View all your stored running data within the app
- **Edit**: Modify or correct any running entries
- **Delete**: Remove individual runs or delete all your data
- **Export**: Export your running data in standard formats

### App Permissions
The app requests minimal permissions:
- **Storage**: To save your running data locally
- **Network**: For optional cloud backup and sync (if enabled)

### Opt-Out Options
- **Analytics**: You can disable anonymous usage analytics in app settings
- **Cloud Sync**: You can choose to keep data local-only

## Data Retention

- **Active Use**: We retain your data as long as you use the app
- **Account Deletion**: When you delete the app, all local data is removed
- **Cloud Data**: If you use cloud backup, data is retained for 30 days after account deletion
- **Anonymous Data**: Anonymous usage statistics may be retained indefinitely

## Security Measures

### Data Protection
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- **Local Storage**: Primary data storage is on your secure device
- **Access Control**: Only you have access to your running data
- **Regular Updates**: We maintain security through regular app updates

### What You Can Do
- **Keep App Updated**: Install app updates promptly
- **Device Security**: Use device lock screens and biometric authentication
- **Backup**: Regularly backup your device to prevent data loss

## Children's Privacy

Vista Running is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- **In-App Notification**: Displaying a notice in the app
- **App Store Updates**: Including policy changes in app update descriptions
- **Effective Date**: The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

## International Users

Vista Running is designed for users worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
